Why Sub Tier Matters More Than Sub Count

A Tier 3 subscriber is worth exactly 5x a Tier 1 subscriber ($24.99 vs $4.99), but most streamers focus purely on growing total sub count rather than upgrading existing subs to higher tiers. Twenty Tier 1 subs generate the same revenue as four Tier 3 subs — but Tier 3 subs tend to come from your most engaged, highest-retention viewers, so they're often more stable month to month.

The 70/30 split is legacy-only. Twitch stopped offering new 70/30 partner deals in 2023. If you're not sure which split applies to you, check your Partner or Affiliate agreement — most streamers today are on the standard 50/50 split regardless of partner status.
