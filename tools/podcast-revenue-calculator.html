How Podcast Advertising Revenue Works

Podcast advertising uses a CPM model (Cost Per Mille) — brands pay per 1,000 downloads per episode. Mid-roll 60-second reads typically earn 30–50% more than pre-roll spots.

CPM formula: (Downloads ÷ 1,000) × CPM × Ad Slots = Revenue per episode
Example: 25,000 downloads × $30 CPM × 2 ads = $1,500 per episode
