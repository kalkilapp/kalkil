How Instagram Influencer Rates Are Set

Instagram doesn't pay creators per view the way YouTube does. Income comes from brand partnerships — and rates depend on follower count, engagement rate, niche, and content format.

Industry baseline: $100 per 10,000 followers — then adjust based on engagement rate and niche. High engagement can double or triple your base rate.
