How to Price Your Influencer Sponsorships

Influencer rates are not set by platforms — they're negotiated directly between creators and brands. The standard industry formula is $100 per 10,000 followers, then adjusted for engagement rate, niche, platform, and content format.

Key principle: engagement quality beats follower quantity. A 50K account with 8% engagement commands higher rates than a 500K account with 0.5% engagement.
