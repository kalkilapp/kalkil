How YouTube Sponsorship Rates Are Set

YouTube sponsorship rates are negotiated directly between creators and brands — YouTube is not involved. Rates depend on subscriber count, average views, audience quality (engagement rate), content niche, and integration type.

Industry baseline: $100 per 10,000 subscribers — then adjust up or down for engagement, niche, and integration type.
