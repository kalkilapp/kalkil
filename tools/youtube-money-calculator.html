How Much Does YouTube Pay Per View?

YouTube revenue depends mainly on CPM (Cost Per 1000 Views). Most creators earn between:

  • $1 – $5 per 1,000 views
  • $1,000 – $5,000 per 1 million views

Your actual revenue depends on several factors:

  • Audience location
  • Video topic (finance pays more)
  • Ad engagement
  • Ad blockers
