How Much Does TikTok Pay Per View?

TikTok monetization mainly comes from the Creator Fund, brand sponsorships, and live gifts.

The Creator Fund usually pays approximately:

  • $0.02 – $0.05 per 1,000 views
  • $20 – $50 per 1 million views

However, most creators earn significantly more through brand deals and influencer marketing partnerships.
