How TikTok Creator Income Actually Works

TikTok pays creators through the Creator Rewards Program at roughly $0.02–$0.04 per 1,000 views — far less than YouTube. But sponsorships can pay 10–100× more than platform rewards for the same audience.

Most TikTok creators earn 90%+ of their income from brand deals, not Creator Rewards. Platform payments are a supplement, not a business model.
