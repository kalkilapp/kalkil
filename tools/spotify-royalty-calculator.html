How Much Does Spotify Pay Per Stream?

Spotify typically pays between $0.003 and $0.005 per stream. This means artists usually earn around:

  • $3 – $5 per 1,000 streams
  • $3,000 – $5,000 per 1 million streams

Actual earnings depend on factors such as listener location, record label agreements and Spotify's royalty pool.
