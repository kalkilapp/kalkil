How Spotify Royalties Work

Spotify uses a pro-rata revenue model: your share of the monthly royalty pool equals your share of total platform streams. There's no fixed per-stream rate — it fluctuates monthly based on Spotify's total revenue and total streams.

Average: $0.003–$0.005 per stream — but a US Premium stream is worth $0.006–$0.010 while a free-tier stream from India earns $0.0003–$0.001.
