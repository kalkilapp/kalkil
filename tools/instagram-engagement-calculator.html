What Is Instagram Engagement Rate?

Engagement rate measures how actively your audience interacts with your content. It's the single most important metric brands check before agreeing to a sponsorship — often more important than raw follower count.

Standard ER = (Likes + Comments) ÷ Followers × 100
Extended ER adds saves and shares for a more complete view.
