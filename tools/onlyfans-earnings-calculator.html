How OnlyFans Revenue Works

OnlyFans creators keep 80% of all revenue — subscriptions, Pay-Per-View messages, tips, and custom content. OnlyFans retains 20%. This is one of the best creator revenue splits in the industry.

80% creator share — better than YouTube (55%), Twitch (50%), and most other platforms. The business model rewards direct fan relationships.
