TikTok Creator Fund Payments Explained

From the original Creator Fund to the new Creator Rewards Program — here's what you actually earn.

Home› Guides› TikTok Creator Fund Payments Explained

TikTok's Creator Fund launched in 2020 with a $200M pool but disappointed creators with payouts of just $0.002–$0.004 per 1,000 views. In 2023, the platform replaced it with the Creator Rewards Program, which pays up to 20× more by rewarding quality, originality, and engagement over raw view counts.

What the Creator Rewards Program Measures

Unlike a simple CPM, the Creator Rewards Program uses a dynamic formula:

  • Video views — the base volume metric
  • Watch time / completion rate — longer views = higher reward
  • Engagement quality — comments and shares weighted more than likes
  • Originality score — penalizes duplicated or trend-copied content
  • Audience location — US and European viewers generate higher payouts

Eligibility Requirements (2026)

  • Age: 18 years or older
  • Minimum 10,000 followers
  • At least 100,000 video views in the past 30 days
  • Account in good standing (no active violations)
  • Available in: US, UK, France, Germany, Brazil, Japan, South Korea, and others
  • Personal account only (not a business account)
