Creator Income Distribution

  • Top 1% of creators earn 40–50% of total platform revenue
  • Top 10% earn 75–80% of total revenue
  • Median creator earns under $1,000/month across most platforms
  • Full-time creator income ($50K+/year): only 2–4% of active creators

The power law distribution doesn't mean success is impossible — it means strategy matters more than luck. Creators who diversify income streams and choose high-value niches disproportionately break into the top tier.
