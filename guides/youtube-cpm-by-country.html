CPM Differences Explained

Three forces drive the gap between high and low CPM countries:

  1. Advertiser demand: Brands pay more to reach consumers who can afford their products.
  2. Ad market maturity: Established digital ad ecosystems create more bidder competition, raising prices.
  3. Content verticals: Finance, insurance, and SaaS advertisers — dominant in wealthy markets — carry the highest CPMs.

How to Attract Higher-CPM Audiences

  • Create content in English to capture US, UK, and Australian viewers.
  • Post at times when US and European audiences are active (evenings EST).
  • Choose topics popular in finance, tech, and business — high-CPM advertiser categories.
  • Use titles and thumbnails with broad English-language appeal.
