Ad Revenue Model

Four ad formats generate creator revenue:

  1. Skippable in-stream ads — creator earns when viewer watches 30+ seconds or clicks.
  2. Non-skippable ads — viewer must watch; generates 20–40% higher CPM.
  3. Bumper ads — 6-second non-skippable; lower CPM but high frequency.
  4. Display & overlay ads — banner formats alongside the video player.

Videos over 8 minutes can include mid-roll ads — typically the single biggest driver of per-video revenue.

Shorts Revenue

Shorts uses a pooled revenue model where ads between videos are shared across creators based on view share. Creators receive 45% of Shorts ad revenue. RPM ranges from $0.03 to $0.07 — best used for channel growth rather than direct income.
