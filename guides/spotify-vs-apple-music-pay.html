Spotify vs Apple Music Pay Per Stream

Apple Music pays 2–3× more per stream than Spotify. Here's the full comparison.

Home› Guides› Spotify vs Apple Music Pay Per Stream

The two biggest paid streaming platforms pay very differently. Apple Music pays roughly $0.007–$0.010 per stream — approximately 2–3× more than Spotify's $0.003–$0.005. Understanding why, and which to prioritize, is essential for independent artists.

Why Apple Music Pays More

  • No free tier: 100% of listeners are paying subscribers, creating a larger royalty pool per stream.
  • User-centric lean: Apple's model more closely ties each listener's subscription fee to the artists they actually play.
  • Higher subscription price: Apple Music charges $10.99/month vs. Spotify's $9.99/month.
