Average Spotify Pay Per Stream

The real numbers behind Spotify's per-stream rates — and what affects your specific payout.

Home› Guides› Average Spotify Pay Per Stream

In 2026, the average Spotify pay per stream is $0.003 to $0.005 — translating to $3–$5 per 1,000 streams. But this average conceals wide variation based on listener geography, subscription tier, and distributor arrangement.

How to Maximize Your Per-Stream Rate

  • Build audience in premium markets (US, UK, Germany, Nordics) — streams pay 5–10× more.
  • Target Premium subscribers through playlist placements and editorial pitching.
  • Distribute to all platforms via a reputable distributor — Apple Music and Tidal pay 2–3× more per stream.
  • Register with a PRO to collect mechanical royalties separately.
