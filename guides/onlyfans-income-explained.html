OnlyFans Earnings Explained

The honest economics of OnlyFans — what creators actually earn and what drives the gap.

Home› Guides› OnlyFans Earnings Explained

OnlyFans has become one of the most financially significant tools for independent creators. In 2026, it hosts over 4 million creators and has paid out billions in creator earnings. The platform keeps 20%, and creators keep 80% — one of the best revenue share rates in the creator economy.

The median OnlyFans creator earns approximately $150–$300/month. The income distribution is highly skewed — top creators drive the vast majority of platform revenue.

What Drives the Biggest Income Gaps

  • External traffic: Creators who actively drive traffic from TikTok, Instagram, X (Twitter), and Reddit significantly outperform those relying on OnlyFans discovery.
  • PPV strategy: Strategic PPV messaging to existing subscribers is the highest-leverage earning tactic on the platform.
  • Pricing optimization: Lower subscription prices ($4.99–$9.99) drive more subscribers; higher prices ($20–$49.99) earn more per subscriber. Test both.
  • Post frequency: Higher-frequency, consistent creators retain subscribers better and reduce churn.
