Affiliate Marketing

Instagram is highly effective for affiliate marketing, especially in fashion, beauty, fitness, and home niches. Use link-in-bio tools (Linktree, Beacons), Story link stickers, and Instagram's native affiliate program.

Best programs: LTK (fashion/lifestyle), Amazon Associates (1–10%), individual brand programs (10–30%).

Instagram Subscriptions

Charge followers $0.99–$99.99/month for exclusive Reels, Stories, Lives, and posts. A creator with 100K followers converting 1% at $4.99/month earns $4,990/month recurring.

Digital Products

Instagram audiences buy: online courses ($97–$997), presets and templates ($15–$150), ebooks ($9–$49), and 1-on-1 coaching. These are zero-inventory, high-margin products that scale without additional effort.
