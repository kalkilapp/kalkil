How Much Does TikTok Pay Per View?

Platform payments are just the beginning. Here's the complete TikTok income picture.

Home› Guides› How Much Does TikTok Pay Per View?

TikTok's Creator Rewards Program pays approximately $0.02–$0.04 per 1,000 views — a fraction of YouTube's RPM. A video with 1 million views earns just $20–$40 from the platform itself. But that same video might unlock brand sponsorships worth $2,000–$10,000. The real TikTok income story is about what views enable, not what they directly pay.

Key insight: TikTok creators with 500K followers typically earn 90–95% of their income from brand deals, affiliate marketing, and digital products — not from Creator Rewards.
