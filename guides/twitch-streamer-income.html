Twitch Affiliate vs. Partner

Affiliate (easier to reach): 50 followers, 500 total minutes broadcast, 7 unique broadcast days, 3 concurrent viewers. Unlocks subscriptions and bits.

Partner (harder threshold): Requires consistent 75+ average viewers, 25 hours streamed, 12 unique stream days in 30 days. Unlocks higher ad revenue and priority support.

Final Thoughts

Twitch rewards community depth over broadcast reach. A streamer with 500 loyal subscribers earns more predictably than one with 5,000 occasional viewers. Build the community first — the monetization follows naturally through subscriptions, bits, and brand partnerships.
