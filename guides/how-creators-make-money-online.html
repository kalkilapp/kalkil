Affiliate Marketing — The Evergreen Stream

Content that earns commissions indefinitely from old posts and videos is the most passive income stream available. Focus on products with recurring commissions: SaaS tools (20–50% monthly), financial products ($50–$500 per conversion), and digital products (30–50%).
