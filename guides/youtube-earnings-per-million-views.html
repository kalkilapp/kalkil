One million views means very different things on different channels. Here's the real math.

CPM vs RPM Explained

CPM (Cost Per Mille) — the gross rate advertisers pay YouTube per 1,000 ad impressions.
RPM (Revenue Per Mille) — what the creator actually receives per 1,000 total views, after YouTube's 45% cut.

RPM ≈ CPM × 0.55 × monetization rate (typically 0.75–0.90)

How to Increase Revenue Per Million Views

  • Enable all ad formats including non-skippable and mid-roll ads (8+ min videos).
  • Target high-income audiences in the US, UK, Canada, and Germany.
  • Add finance or tech elements to your content to attract higher-paying advertisers.
  • Post consistently during Q4 when advertiser CPMs spike 30–80%.
  • Improve average watch time — more minutes watched means more ad impressions per view.
