Why Micro-Influencers Often Outperform Macro

Brands increasingly prefer micro-influencers (10K–100K) for ROI: higher engagement rates (3–8% vs 0.5–2%), greater audience trust, and lower cost per engaged viewer. A budget allocated to 10 micro-influencers frequently outperforms one macro deal.
