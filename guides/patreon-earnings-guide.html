Tier Pricing Strategy

  • Entry tier ($1–$3): Low barrier, basic supporter recognition. Goal: volume.
  • Core tier ($5–$15): Exclusive content, behind-the-scenes, early access. Goal: primary revenue driver.
  • Premium tier ($25–$100+): 1-on-1 access, Discord calls, physical goods. Goal: superfan conversion.

Most Patreon revenue comes from the middle tier. Premium tiers convert only 5–10% of total patrons but generate disproportionate revenue. Always offer all three.

Frequently Asked Questions

Patrons are charged on the 1st of each month for monthly memberships, or immediately upon joining for per-creation billing. Creators receive payouts once monthly after a processing period.
There's no minimum — some creators earn meaningful income with 50–100 highly engaged patrons. What matters more than total audience size is how deeply your existing audience trusts and values your work.
Patreon works best for creators with a loyal, niche audience: podcasters, musicians, writers, educators, and comics artists see the strongest conversion rates. It's less effective for broad entertainment channels with casual viewers.
